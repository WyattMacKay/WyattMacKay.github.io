My Games

Break the Blatzi

My second game. This is a Valentine's gift to my girlfriend of five years.

The ball that you beat up is named Blatzi, it is her original character. The third level has her favorite Pokemon named Armarouge.

I made everything in this game besides the sound effects, and I'm proud of how it turned out. This was my first time working with sound, and I think it turned out well.

Don't Drop Gary

My first game. I made this game in a week for the University of New Brunswick Video Game Development 2024 Summer Camp that I was a volunteer for. There I had the opportunity to help teach local middle school students about the Godot game engine. I had a lot of fun and learnt a lot myself.

This game is inspired by old mobile games, like Angry Birds. It consists of eight levels and a "thank you" bonus.
