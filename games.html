Don't Drop Gary

My first game. I made this game in a week for the University of New Brunswick Video Game Development 2024 Summer Camp that I was a volunteer for. There I had the opportunity to help teach local middle school students about the Godot game engine. I had a lot of fun and learnt a lot myself.

This game is inspired by old mobile games, like Angry Birds. It consists of eight levels and a "thank you" bonus.
